Polyelectrolyte Complex Film Dressing

Polymer-Based Wound Dressing for Controlled Bioactive Delivery

Chronic skin wounds are epithelial injuries, requiring wound dressings that support epithelial regeneration and sustained signaling. We developed a polymer-based polyelectrolyte complex (PEC) film dressing designed for controlled delivery of bioactive nanoparticles, like MSH-…ë to promote wound healing.

The PEC films, composed of biocompatible natural polymers, enable slow, graded release of elastin-like polypeptide (ELP) based growth factor nanoparticles over physiologically relevant timeframes. Swelling, degradation, and release studies demonstrated tunable stability and sustained delivery under wound-mimicking conditions.
