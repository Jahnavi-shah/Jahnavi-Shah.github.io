Biocomposite Scaffold for Articular Cartilage Regeneration

The scaffold that will be engineered to support mesenchymal stem cell chondrogenesis while providing patient-specific, tunable degradation profiles.

In the design, synthetic cannabinoids would be incorporated to reduce inflammation, enhance stem cell survival, and promote chondrocyte differentiation through endocannabinoid signaling pathways. The design integrates biomaterials engineering, cell biology, and controlled drug delivery principles.

A bioprinting-based fabrication workflow was proposed to enable spatial control over scaffold architecture and mechanical properties, along with an evaluation strategy combining molecular, histological, and mechanical testing to assess regenerative outcomes.
